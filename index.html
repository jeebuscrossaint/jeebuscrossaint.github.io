** welcome! **

hey! i'm amarnath — physics & mathematics student at UCF, programmer, linux nerd, and occasional tinkerer with things that blink or beep. this is my little corner of the internet.

i work on instrumentation software at UCF CREOL's astrophotonics lab and on physics education research with the UCF physics department. mostly i write rust and python and break my arch install on a weekly basis.

this site is hand-coded HTML/CSS/JS — no frameworks, no npm, no build step. you can read my writing, grab my résumé, or just look at the badges.

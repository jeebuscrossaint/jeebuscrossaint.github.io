Illuminate

Welcome to Illuminate

A virtual hackathon designed for high-schoolers to showcase their coding skills and creativity. Join us for an exciting weekend of coding challenges, workshops, and networking opportunities. Anything green is important!

But what is, Illuminate?

Illuminate is a hackathon oriented towards high-schoolers to allow students to display their programming abilities and computational intelligence along perhaps with their artistic creativity. Organized by Code the Stars, prepare for a full weekend with enjoyable coding tasks, workshops, and networking opportunities. Join us in an event where you can interact with other students from people around the world, and put your skills ot the test!

Are there prizes?

There should be prizes, however it depends on who sponsors the hackathon, according to some organizers they say they would be about 90% sure to get cool prizes. Their target is supposedly $2000 USD.

No, in hackathons you typically create a project, so it is rather creative. Real hacking competitions are usually CTFs which strictly enforce legal jurisdiction.
